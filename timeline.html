Deadpool's Mutations

How the "Merc with a Mouth" turned from a derrivative gritty nobody to a worldwide success.

Deadpool, the violent and irreverant mutant mercenary, broke records for R-rated movies and is already shifting the superhero film aesthetic, despite its meager budget and tumoltuous production.

But while his 4th-wall breaking, pop-culture referencing, limbs ripping journey on the big screen is just beginning, he already had quite a ride in comics.
